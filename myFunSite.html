About

We created this fitness group that provides everyone with basic knowledge of how to get started with their fitness journey. We organized this fitness page according to what we think would have helped us a lot in the early stages of our fitness journey.

Often times people are discouraged to start their fitness journey because of various of reasons such as: it's out of their comfort zone, they don't know where to start, they don't think it can fit in with their schedule, and etc. It's always easier when there's a team backing you up and encouraging you. We are here to help people get started. We are here to share our knowledge based off of our experiences and encourage everyone in the community to do so as well.
